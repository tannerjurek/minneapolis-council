How to view this site

  1. From the repo root, run python3 -m http.server 8000.
  2. Open http://localhost:8000/site/ in your browser.
  3. Click Load real agenda sample or upload your reviewed manual_segments.csv.

The included real agenda sample has one substantive item. Add timestamped meeting segments to switch the chart from item counts to minutes.
